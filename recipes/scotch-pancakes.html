Scotch Pancakes

Description

These are just normal Pancakes except there is Scotch mixed into the batter. They don't taste good, but they do make you feel good.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Syrup
  • Scotch

Steps

  1. Put flour, egg, milk, and Scotch in a bowl and mix them around until it feels like pancake batter. Try to maximize Scotch.
  2. Put some butter on a hot frying pan and pour the batter on it.
  3. Cook until it feels like pancakes.

If you put too much Scotch in them, as sometimes will happen, the pancakes will be mushy and drip with Scotch. It's not pleasant, but don't waste the scotch.
