Dagwood Sandwich

Description

A favorite of Grandpa Schirmer's after a morning of fishing on the lake, inspired by a comic he used to read back in the 40's.

Ingredients

  • 4 or more slices of wonderbread or equivalent.
  • peanut butter
  • tomato slices
  • lettuce
  • at least two different kinds of deli meat
  • at least two different kinds of deli cheese
  • mayonnaise
  • mustard
  • pickles
  • onion
  • salt and pepper
  • any other optional ingredients that can fit between two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Make a normal sandwich using first kind of cheese and deli meat chosen, together with choice of condiments.
  2. Make a second normal sandwich with the second kind of cheese and deli meat, together with choice of condiments.
  3. Smear layer of peanut butter on the top of first sandwich.
  4. Embed tomato slices into peanut butter layer, salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Smear layer of peanut butter on bottom of second sandwich.
  6. Stack second sandwich on top of first sandwich.
  7. Repeat as necessary until all bread and fixings have been used.

Warnings: These are the minimum meat, cheese, and bread specifications of a Dagwood sandwich, and it is expected that every condiment should occur at least once in the sandwich. For structural reasons, it is essential that the peanut-butter tomato layer occur in the center of the sandwich. Larger stacks are possible, but even the minimal Dagwood sandwich demands considerable eating skill to maintain to maintain its structural integrity while it is being consumed.
